PHOTO WALK —
PRÍNCIPE REAL

GALLERY ANCHORED TOUR

An exclusive photographic walk through Príncipe Real, beginning and ending at Alta Photo Lisboa and Alta Records Lisboa — a physical cultural space where photography, rare vinyl and Lisbon’s atmosphere converge.

The walk is intentionally slow and curated, focused on light, framing, silence and cinematic streets rather than touristic routes. A visual experience anchored in place, mood and authorship.
