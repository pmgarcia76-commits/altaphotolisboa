PHOTO WALK _
PRÍNCIPE REAL

Gallery anchored tour

An intimate, author-driven photo walk in Príncipe Real, one of Lisbon's most culturally active and visually distinct neighbourhoods.

Conceived and led by Pedro, founder of Alta Photo Lisboa, a photographer with over 25 years of practice across editorial, commercial and conceptual work.

During the walk, each participant is photographed as the central subject of a personal editorial, becoming the model and protagonist of the narrative.

Alta Photo Lisboa shares space with Alta Records Lisboa, home to a vinyl collection built over 35 years by Sérgio, a lifelong collector and DJ.

Quiet streets, everyday spaces, shops, cafés and Praça das Flores define the walk — places shaped by local life rather than tourism.

Each participant receives one printed ½ photography, printed and signed by the photographer. The complete editorial selection is shared privately via download link.

Duration: approximately 2 hours.

Price per participant: 150 €.
