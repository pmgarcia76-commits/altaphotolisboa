PRÍNCIPE REAL
PHOTO WALK

gallery anchored tour

An intimate, author-driven photo walk in Príncipe Real, Lisbon's most culturally active and visually distinct neighbourhood.

Conceived and led by Pedro, photographer and founder of Alta Photo Lisboa, each participant is photographed as the central subject of a personal editorial. The participant becomes the model and protagonist of the narrative.

The walk starts at Alta Photo Lisboa, shared with Alta Records Lisboa — home to a 35-year vinyl collection curated by Sérgio, lifelong collector and DJ.

Quiet streets, everyday spaces, shops, cafés and Praça das Flores shape the walk — places defined by local life rather than tourism.

Each participant receives one printed 1/2 photography, printed and signed by the photographer. The full editorial is shared privately via download link.

Duration: approximately 2 hours.
Price per participant: 150 €.
