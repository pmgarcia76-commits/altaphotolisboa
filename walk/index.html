PHOTO WALK —
PRÍNCIPE REAL

GALLERY ANCHORED TOUR

An exclusive photographic walk through Príncipe Real, beginning and ending at Alta Photo Lisboa and Alta Records Lisboa — a physical cultural space anchored in photography, rare vinyl and Lisbon’s atmosphere.

The walk is slow, curated and visually driven, focusing on light, framing and cinematic streets rather than touristic routes. A gallery-anchored experience in the heart of Príncipe Real.
