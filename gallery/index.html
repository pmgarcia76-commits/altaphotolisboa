Hidden in Príncipe Real, @alta.photo.lisboa exists side by side with @alta.records.lisboa

Founded and conceived by Pedro — a photographer and curator with 25 years of experience, and Sergio, a 35‑year vinyl collector, curator, and DJ — the space is a quiet hub for exhibitions, 1/1 prints, workshops, micro-venues, and photo walks through the neighborhood. The impressive collection sets the tone: original pressings, first editions, impossible records, and deep listening.

A gallery, a record sanctuary, a hidden cultural spot.
Welcome to Alta.
